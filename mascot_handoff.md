- **3D cartoon/Pixar style** — round, friendly, approachable
- **Googley eyes**: Large, white, slightly mismatched/goofy with black pupils
- **Smile**: Friendly, open-mouth or closed-mouth grin — think Pixar's *Luxo Ball*
- **Transparent background** (PNG with alpha channel) — NO background color, shadow, or floor
- **Centered** in frame, facing slightly toward camera
- **Consistent lighting** across all 6 — soft studio light from upper-left
- **Square aspect ratio** recommended (1:1), at least 512×512px
