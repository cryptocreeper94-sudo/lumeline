Our Commitment

LumeLine believes in transparency — not just in odds data, but in our responsibility to users. While LumeLine is an analytics platform (not a gambling service), we recognize that the sports betting industry can carry risks, and we take that seriously.

Know the Signs

If you or someone you know is struggling with problem gambling, please be aware of these signs:

  • Spending more money than you can afford to lose
  • Chasing losses or increasing bet sizes to recover
  • Feeling anxious, stressed, or irritable about gambling
  • Neglecting responsibilities, work, or relationships
  • Lying about gambling activity or hiding losses
  • Borrowing money to gamble

Our Platform Safeguards

  • We do not accept wagers or facilitate any gambling activity
  • We do not partner with or promote any gambling operators
  • Our consensus predictions always include confidence scores — low-confidence games are flagged, not promoted
  • House lean detection and anomaly alerts help users make more informed decisions
  • We will never use dark patterns or gamification to encourage risky behavior
